Decentralised Lottery
● Players can enter the lottery by paying a certain amount
● Generate a random winner. (optional for you to give the hint: can do using chain link VRF).
● Transfer all the amount deposited to the winner.
● Make it automated generating winners and distributing funds after a fixed time. ( hint: can do using chain link keepers).
